02 About APLs

Step Into the Standard

Access measurable frameworks for image consulting. Receive evidence-backed direction from certified practitioners across Korea, Japan, and China.

A quiet standard, built deliberately.

APLs was founded in December 2024 as a Seoul-based corporation dedicated to replacing subjective beauty judgment with measurable frameworks. We operate three specialized sub-brands under one governance structure. Our work spans individual diagnosis, professional certification, and enterprise consulting.

03 How We Work

From Observation to Standard

Step 01

Measure

Calibrated colorimeters capture LAB values from skin, hair, and iris. No subjective judgment enters the process.

→
Step 02

Analyze

Data is mapped against our 13,000+ record database using proprietary algorithms to identify patterns and classifications.

→
Step 03

Consult

KCFB-certified practitioners integrate color, face shape, and body analysis into a unified image strategy.

→
Step 04

Set Standard

A personalized reference document is delivered: measurable, reproducible, and independent of any single consultant.
